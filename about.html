Our mission, vision and values

We are Lively, created by three BUas students, Fatoumata Diakhaby, Iris Severens and Thirze Rust all living in Breda. With Lively we want to celebrate and encourage all the vibrant and colorful people in the world, which in our opinion is everyone! Our Design Justifications

Our Brand focusses on creating a community and embracing values like Individuality, Peace and Respect. We want to create an online space for artists worldwide to come together and share the beauty that is art. The style of our brand focusses on the uniqueness and vibrancy of every artists style and therefore has a bright colour palette, with unusual colors that are created on an artists’ pallet. The main colors for our brand are yellow, blue and a peachy pink/purple. But in the use of our many images and backgrounds we show the variety of color combinations we as a community want to embrace. We do this because we feel like as supporters of artists and all people in general, every colour of the spectrum should be celebrated. We want people to feel comfortable in their own skin, judging eachother won’t make that easier for any of us. We are here to break that barriere together. Live and let live!
