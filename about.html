Our Design Justifications

Our Brand focusses on creating a community and embracing values like Individuality, Peace and Respect. We want to create an online space for artists worldwide to come together and share the beauty that is art. The style of our brand focusses on the uniqueness of every artists style and therefore has a bright colour palette, with unusual colors that are created on an artistsâ€™ pallet. The main colors for our brand are yellow, blue and a peachy pink/purple. But in the use of our many images and backgrounds we show the variety of color combinations we as a community want to embrace. We do this because we feel like as supporters of artists, every colour of the spectrum should be celebrated. ABOUT LIVELY

Lively is a fictional online artist community made for a project for Breda University of Applied Sciences. However, Lively is a very inspiring brand that wants to create a community and supports individuality, respect and peace.
